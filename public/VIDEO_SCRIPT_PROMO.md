## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Cursor Movement**: Move slowly and deliberately - viewers need time to follow
2. **Pauses**: Add 1-second pauses before and after important actions
3. **Clean Clicks**: Single, confident clicks - avoid double-clicking
4. **Scroll Speed**: Slow, smooth scrolling - no jerky movements
5. **Multiple Takes**: Record each section separately for easier editing
6. **B-Roll**: Record extra footage of the UI for cutaway options
