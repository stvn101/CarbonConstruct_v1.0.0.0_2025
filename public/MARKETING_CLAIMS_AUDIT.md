## Legal Compliance Notes

### Australian Consumer Law (ACL)
- Environmental claims must be truthful, accurate, and able to be substantiated
- Vague claims like "eco-friendly" without evidence can be misleading
- Quantified claims (percentages, savings) require documentation

### ACCC Eight Principles for Environmental Claims
1. ‚úÖ Make accurate and truthful claims
2. ‚ö†Ô∏è Have evidence to back up claims (ROI needs work)
3. ‚ö†Ô∏è Be specific about benefits (progress metrics vague)
4. ‚úÖ Don't exaggerate or overstate benefits
5. ‚úÖ Use clear and easy-to-understand language
6. ‚úÖ Explain how a product/service delivers environmental benefits
7. ‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid using absolute claims (99% accuracy)
8. ‚úÖ Be transparent about limitations
